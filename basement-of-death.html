Basement Of Death

Horror • Puzzle Solving • Escape Room (VR)

The Mission

Create an immersive VR horror escape room that demonstrates advanced Unity development skills and compelling narrative design. The brief required a fully playable experience with environmental storytelling and a mind-bending twist.

My Role

Lead Developer, Narrative Designer & Level Designer

Architected the entire game in Unity using C# scripting, designed 4 distinct levels with thematically connected puzzles, and crafted the Human vs AI narrative twist that questions player identity.

The Tech

Unity C# VR Development XR Toolkit ProBuilder Physics 3D Audio

The Result

Delivered a 2-3 hour gameplay experience with positive playtesting feedback. Players particularly praised the narrative twist and puzzle variety, showcasing my ability to handle narrative design, level design, puzzle mechanics, and VR interaction programming.

The Human vs AI Twist

The core narrative explores the blurred line between human consciousness and artificial intelligence. Through environmental clues scattered across 4 levels, the player gradually discovers they are actually 'Human_AI_920'—a Human-AI hybrid experiment created by billionaire Jeffrey Campbell. The game's puzzles are specifically designed to test cognitive abilities that distinguish human thinking from AI logic, forcing players to question their own identity as they progress through the mansion.

Puzzle Design Breakdown

Level 1 - Lockpicking

Physics-based hairpin mechanic introducing VR interaction

Level 2 - Logic Keypad

Cognitive test using human knowledge (months=12, spider legs=8)

Level 3 - The Doll

Physical manipulation puzzle revealing a hidden key

Level 4 - Chemical Sorting

Color-coded test tube puzzle based on cryptic poetry

Engineering Challenges

Locomotion System

Implemented teleportation-based movement to reduce VR motion sickness, ensuring comfortable 90fps performance for extended play sessions.

Puzzle Logic

Coded the spider-leg/month logic puzzle system to test player cognition, creating a dynamic challenge that questions human vs AI thinking patterns.

Performance Optimization

Reduced rendering overhead by 40% through occlusion culling, LOD systems, and material batching to maintain VR comfort.

Spawn System Debug

Fixed race condition in scene transitions by implementing coroutine-based spawn validation, ensuring reliable player positioning.
